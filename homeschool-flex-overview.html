How Homeschool Flex Works

Homeschool Flex is designed to be simple first and powerful second. You can use the app at the level you need — fully manual or fully structured with AI assistance.

Start With Only the Basics

Adding a student requires only name, age, and grade level. No forced schedules. No required active days. No unnecessary setup.

This allows families to begin quickly without being blocked by configuration steps.

Manual or Structured Scheduling

Manual Scheduling

Select a student, select a subject, add notes, and save. The system still tracks grades and performance.

Structured Scheduling

Activate school days for a student and schedule full workbooks at once. Lessons are placed only on active school days.

Learn more about detailed scheduling: Homeschool Scheduling Guide

Subjects and Workbooks

Subjects

Create a subject with only subject name, category, and grade level. Students and subjects do not have to match grade levels.

Workbooks

Add lessons, quizzes, and tests in order. Once created, a workbook can be scheduled all at once.

Learn more: Subjects and Workbooks Guide

AI Scheduling and Rescheduling

Use AI to create lesson schedules or adjust plans when life changes. The AI respects active school days and existing lessons.

Examples:

  • “Schedule 20 math lessons for Jim starting next week.”
  • “Jim is sick today. Move today’s lessons to Mondays.”

Learn more: AI Scheduling Guide

Attendance Tracking

Attendance is marked manually to prevent inaccurate reports. You can also update previous days if needed.

Attendance Guide

Reading Log

Register books and log student reading progress in a simple reading log.

Grades and Reports

Add grades manually or automatically when marking tests completed. Generate reports including quizzes or tests only.

Expense Tracker

Track homeschool expenses using only name, amount, and category. No complex financial setup required.

Community

Join anonymous homeschool groups such as large families or ADHD families. Post, comment, and participate in a moderated, family-safe environment.

Household and Tutor Mode

Invite parents, students, or tutors to your household. Tutors can activate Tutor Mode and view assigned students in one schedule view.
