Homeschool Scheduling Guide

Homeschool Flex allows you to schedule lessons in multiple ways. You can keep it fully manual, structured, or AI-assisted. The system adapts to your workflow instead of forcing one.

For a full overview of the app, see: How Homeschool Flex Works

Two Scheduling Foundations

1. Manual Scheduling

Manual scheduling is the simplest method. Select a student, choose a subject, add notes, and save.

This works well for:

  • Custom lesson planning
  • Independent pacing
  • Flexible daily adjustments

Even when scheduling manually, grades and test performance are still tracked.

2. Structured Scheduling with Active School Days

When active school days are set for a student, the system can automatically place lessons only on those selected days.

If no school days are active, bulk scheduling will not occur. This prevents unintended lesson placement.

Scheduling Workbooks

Workbooks allow you to schedule an entire sequence of lessons at once.

After creating a workbook, you can:

  • Select the student
  • Select the workbook
  • Choose a start date
  • Save

The system schedules all lessons on active school days automatically.

Learn how subjects and workbooks are created: Subjects and Workbooks Guide

AI Scheduling

If you do not have a structured workbook, you can describe what you need and let AI create the schedule.

Examples:

  • “Schedule 20 math lessons starting next Monday.”
  • “Give Mary a typing lesson once per week.”
  • “Add an extra reading lesson every Friday.”

The AI respects:

  • Active school days
  • Selected student
  • Existing schedule

Detailed AI instructions: AI Scheduling Guide

AI Rescheduling

When life changes, schedules can be adjusted using AI.

Examples:

  • “Jim is sick today. Move today’s lessons to Mondays.”
  • “Reschedule this week’s lessons one day forward.”

The AI modifies the schedule without affecting unrelated lessons.

Special Events

You can create special events and apply them to specific students. Lessons will not be scheduled on those days.

Schedule Viewer

The schedule viewer allows filtering by:

  • Student
  • Subject
  • Text search

This makes it easy to locate specific lessons quickly.

Printing Schedules

Schedules can be printed when needed for physical records or planning.

Keep Scheduling Simple

Whether you prefer manual planning or AI automation, Homeschool Flex keeps scheduling flexible and controlled.
